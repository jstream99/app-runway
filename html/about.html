How it started

As four high school girls, we often struggled to pick out outfits in a timely manner that were school appropriate but still allowed us to express ourselves and feel confident in our clothing. In the age of Artificial Intelligence and social media, we wanted a novel solution that utilizes technology to help us curate our personal style. That’s why we created Runway: a virtual wardrobe app that curates personalized outfit suggestions based on style preferences, weather conditions, and special occasions.

Our mission

Runway’s mission is to boost your confidence and eliminate outfit indecisiveness by providing a virtual wardrobe and personalized outfit suggestions, saving you time and allowing you to feel comfortable in your outfit decisions.
