How it works

  • Home
  • how it works
style survey

 

The style survey is the first thing that you see when you open Runway. This quick and easy survey that will help us get to know your style preferences so we can suggest the perfect outfit.

 

 

virtual wardrobe

 

This personalized wardrobe stores your existing clothes/accessories and organizes them into convenient category-based folders. Your uploaded items will be displayed in each folder.

 

 

outfit suggestions

 

Once completing the style survey and uploading your clothes to your virtual wardrobe, our AI algorithm will be able to curate the perfect outfit that takes into account the weather, style preferences, preferable color schemes, and more.

 

virtual model

 

Your personalized virtual model will display your body type and allow you to drag and drop clothes from the virtual wardrobe onto the model. Not only will this save you time trying on multiple outfits, but it will also eliminate the alternative of having a giant pile of clothes in your room.
social media feed

 

Runway includes a social media-like feed where you will be able to create and post outfits and see others’ creations as well. Users will be able to tag the brand of the clothing and accessories and also be able to save outfits they like.

 

saved outfits

 

When we suggest an outfit that you love, you can save it for future reference into this folder. Additionally, when you’re scrolling through the social media feed and see can outfit you like, you can save it into this folder as well.
